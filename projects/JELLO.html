uOttawa Japanese Club

Description

Since I was a freshman, I have been involved in the University of Ottawa Japanese club where we do weekly cultural/learning activities related with Japan and their language. I was also an excutif for one year which motivated me to contribute to the club's future and success. I created the main website where we display the club's information. Also, one of the issues we encountered was that at the beginning of the semester, to register the new members' emails into our newsletter list, we collected their email information on a piece a paper. This causes multiple issues: it takes a long time to enter the information and the writing is sometimes difficult to read. This is why I created an application which collects the member's information on a computer and included an export feature where you can copy and paste all the emails at once.

Main Features

  • JELLO's website:
    • About: Club's description and current executives names and pictures
    • News: General information and a Facebook iframe with the latest news
    • Register: Send an automatic email to the club asking to be added to the newsletter list
    • Toggle languages from English and French
    • Responsive UI with bootstrap
  • Registration software:
    • Register a new member (name, email, language level, comments)
    • View all registered members information
    • Export names, emails, language levels and/or comments
