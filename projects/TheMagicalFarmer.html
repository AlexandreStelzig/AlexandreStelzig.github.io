The Magical Farmer

Description

This is an Unity 3D Video Game available on Mac and Windows that was created in one month during a introduction to 3D video game programming class in South Korea.
Game summary:
Once upon a time, there was a small village living in peace. But one day, monsters invaded the land, stealing and destroying everything. Having no choice, the villagers abandoned the land. But there was one person that still had hope: The magical farmer. The player plays as the magical farmer. His main goal is to venture into the dungeon and fight waves of enemies. By defeating the waves, he can collect seeds. Using those seeds, he can grow crops and build new buildings in the city. Each building has their own specialty which will help the player become more powerful and defeat stronger enemies.

Media


Main Features

  • Fight 6 types of enemies using 4 different skills
  • Collect seeds by defeating waves of enemies
  • Plant seeds on your farm to get crops
  • Use the crops to rebuild the village (6 different types of buildings)
  • Become more powerful by interacting with the npcs that appears with the buildings
  • Complete a quest chain to unlock a secret zone
  • Day/Night cycle with dynamic lighting and sound
  • Try to reach the highest level by attempting the 'Infinity Waves' Level
